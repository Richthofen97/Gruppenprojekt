Authenticate to Abstract

Authentication allows users to securely access their accounts and collaborate with their teams.

Abstract supports modern authentication systems such as Single Sign-On (SSO) to simplify access while maintaining strong security standards.

Administrators can configure SCIM provisioning and Just-in-Time user creation to automatically manage team members and permissions.

These features help organizations maintain secure access control while making it easy for teams to sign in and start working immediately.
